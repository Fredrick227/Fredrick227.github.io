FAQ

How long will my order take to ship?

Please allow 2-4 days for us to prepare your garment after you purchase. We promise to always ship it out no later than 7 days after ordering.

There is an issue with my order. What do I do?

Contact us through our Contact page or email us directly at cuthereclub@gmail.com. Rest assured, you can expect an answer from us within 24 hours.

Do you accept returns?

If you aren’t fully happy with the goods you’ve received, you can return them to our warehouse in the USA (Nevada). Please email us first at cuthereclub@gmail.com so we can track the return process. Returns must arrive within 14 days of delivery confirmation for a refund. Products must be in saleable condition when they arrive back (unworn, original tags attached, no marks, unwashed, and no strong odors).

The customer is responsible for return shipping costs. We recommend using a trackable and insured shipping service. We are not responsible for items lost or damaged in transit.

Once we receive the item, you will be refunded within 5–7 business days to the original payment method.

Where do you ship? Are there import fees?

We offer free shipping in the USA for all orders above $150. There are no import fees, and delivery time ranges from 2 to 7 days after ordering.

What is your size guide?

We recommend true size or a slightly oversized look. However, as our sizing is naturally larger, if you prefer a tighter fit, choose one size smaller than your usual size. Each product page includes a detailed size guide to help you find your perfect fit.

How do I care for my clothes?

Wash at a maximum of 40°C (104°F) for tees and sweaters. For more delicate items like jacquard wovens or knitwear, use a gentle 30°C (86°F) wash. Wash with similar colors to avoid dye transfer. If you need advice, feel free to contact us at cuthereclub@gmail.com.

Will you take suggestions for the next drop?

Of course! Feel free to send your suggestions via email to cuthereclub@gmail.com. While we value customer input, we are committed to our unique design vision and aesthetic direction to ensure the integrity of our brand.
